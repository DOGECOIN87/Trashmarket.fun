## Community Airdrop

### How It Works

DEBRIS is being airdropped to community members who register through the official airdrop page on Trashmarket.fun. Registration requires a Twitter/X account and a Gorbagana wallet address.

### Airdrop Tiers

**Standard Airdrop — 0.1% of Total Supply Per User**

- Each registered user receives **1,000,000 DEBRIS**
- Open to all users who complete the registration process
- One registration per Twitter/X account
- Limited supply — once the airdrop pool is exhausted, registration closes

**VIP Airdrop — 1% of Total Supply Per User**

- Select community members receive **10,000,000 DEBRIS** each
- Limited to a maximum of **6 VIP recipients**
- Awarded to key early supporters and contributors

### How to Register

1. Visit the Airdrop page on [Trashmarket.fun](https://trashmarket.fun)
2. Sign in with your Twitter/X account
3. Enter your Gorbagana wallet address
4. Confirm your registration

Your wallet must be a valid Gorbagana address (Base58, 32-44 characters). You can use any Gorbagana-compatible wallet.

## Game Liquidity

A combined **40% of the total supply** is dedicated to game payouts, ensuring that players always have real rewards to earn.

### Junk Pusher (25.0% — 249,975,990 DEBRIS)

The physics-based coin pusher arcade game. Players deposit DEBRIS to play and can withdraw their winnings at any time. A 2.5% platform fee is applied on deposits, which helps recycle tokens back into the ecosystem.

### Slots (14.9% — 148,500,000 DEBRIS)

The upcoming skill-based slots game with memory bonus rounds. This allocation is reserved and will be activated at launch.

---

## DEX Liquidity (14.9% — 148,500,000 DEBRIS)

A significant portion of the supply is allocated to creating and maintaining a DEBRIS/GOR trading pair on the Trashmarket DEX. This ensures that DEBRIS holders can freely trade their tokens at fair market value.

---

## Platform Operations (15.0% — 149,985,000 DEBRIS)

Funds allocated for ongoing platform development, maintenance, and operational costs. This includes infrastructure for games, the NFT marketplace, bridge operations, and ecosystem growth.

---

## Reserve (5.5% — 54,614,114 DEBRIS)

Held in a secure wallet for future use including:

- Additional airdrop rounds if the community grows
- Strategic partnerships
- Emergency liquidity if needed
- Ecosystem expansion initiatives
