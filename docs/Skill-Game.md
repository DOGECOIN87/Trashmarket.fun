- **Currency:** DEBRIS Token
- **Network:** Gorbagana (SVM L2)
- **Play Levels:** 10, 25, 50, 100, 250, 1,000, 2,500, 5,000, or 9,999 DEBRIS per spin

## Gameplay

### The Grid

The game uses a 3x3 grid with 9 unique symbol types. Each symbol has a different value — rarer symbols pay more. The paytable on the left side of the screen shows the exact payout for each symbol at your current Play Level.

### Spinning

When you press **Play**, your wager is deducted and the grid spins. Once the reels stop, you'll see 9 symbols arranged on the grid.

### Placing Your WILD

This is where skill comes in. After the spin, you choose **one cell** to place a WILD symbol. The WILD acts as a substitute — if the other two symbols on a winning line match, the WILD completes the 3-in-a-row and you win.

Your goal: **find the position where your WILD creates the highest-paying match.**

### Winning Lines

There are 8 possible winning lines:

- 3 horizontal rows
- 3 vertical columns
- 2 diagonals

Only the **best single line** pays out per spin. Lines with your WILD need 2 matching symbols. Lines without the WILD can still win if all 3 symbols match naturally.

## Preview System

Before committing your wager, you can **preview** the grid to see what symbols will appear.

- Click **Preview** to reveal the grid for 2 seconds
- You can preview the same grid as many times as you like
- Each Play Level has its own grid — switching levels shows a different grid
- Grids are locked per level until you play them
- After playing, the next preview at that level generates a fresh grid

Use previews to scout across different Play Levels and find the best opportunity before wagering.

## Tips & Strategy

- **Use previews wisely** — Scout multiple Play Levels before committing. Each level has its own grid, so check a few before deciding where to play.
- **Look for pairs** — When previewing, look for two matching high-value symbols on the same line. That's where you'll want to place your WILD.
- **Manage your bankroll** — Start at lower Play Levels to get comfortable with the game, then move up when you're ready.
- **The center cell is powerful** — Cell 5 (center) appears on 4 winning lines (both diagonals, middle row, middle column), giving you more chances to complete a match.
- **Rare symbols pay big** — Alon (25x) and Oscar (8x) are rare but worth waiting for. If you spot a pair during preview, that's your opportunity.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I lose my deposit?**
A: Your game balance decreases when you spin and don't win enough to cover the wager. You can withdraw your remaining balance at any time.

**Q: What happens if I refresh the page?**
A: Your game balance is saved and will be restored when you reconnect your wallet. Previewed grids are also preserved.

**Q: Is the game rigged?**
A: No. Outcomes are determined by a weighted random system before the grid is constructed. Your skill in placing the WILD determines whether you maximize or miss the payout. The house edge is approximately 9%, comparable to standard arcade and slot games.

**Q: What's the maximum I can win?**
A: The highest payout is 25x your wager. At Play Level 9,999, that's **249,975 DEBRIS** on a single spin.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes. The game is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
